Rationale

We chose blue and dark turquoise as our main colours because they bring feelings of calmness and relaxation just like the sky and sea. These feelings represent our app and how it helps with mental health. In addition, spiritually, blue is associated with mental health and connecting to the universe. Blue is also said to bring positive effects on the mind and body.

As for our logo and mascot colours, we went for earthy colours, like yellow, green, and brown. These colours represent nature, which is exactly what our logo and mascot are about. We wanted to have these colours in our app to help showcase warmth, reassurance, and to be soothing.
