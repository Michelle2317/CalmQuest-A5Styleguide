Logo Design

Rationale

For our choice of shapes, we choose a radiating sunflower to represent happiness, positivity, and hope. It also matches our name “CalmQuest” because sunflowers always follows the sun, it could be seen as looking forward with hope or away from the shadows that plague us.

For our font choice, we used Open Sans because it has excellent legibility and is optimized for print, web, and mobile interfaces. It also gives off a neutral and friendly appearance.
